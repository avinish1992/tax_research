## Design Principles

### 1. Clarity Over Cleverness
- Legal and tax content is complex enough; the interface should be crystal clear
- Use plain language in UI elements
- Provide context and guidance where needed

### 2. Professional & Trustworthy
- Convey competence and reliability through design
- Use subtle, sophisticated color palette
- Maintain consistency across all touchpoints

### 3. Efficiency First
- Minimize clicks to accomplish tasks
- Surface relevant actions contextually
- Support keyboard shortcuts for power users

### 4. Intelligent Assistance
- AI should feel helpful, not intrusive
- Show confidence levels transparently
- Make AI reasoning visible when appropriate

### 5. Calm Technology
- Reduce visual noise and distraction
- Use color purposefully for emphasis
- Create focus through whitespace
