# Go-To-Market Strategy: Legal AI Tax Research Platform

> Last Updated: December 2025

## Executive Summary

The legal tech market reached **$4.3B in 2025** (up 54% from 2024), while tax tech is projected to hit **$60.66B by 2034**. AI adoption in accounting firms jumped from 9% to 41% in one year. Major VCs including Andreessen Horowitz and Sequoia are aggressively funding AI-legal startups (Harvey at $8B valuation). This creates a significant window for an AI-native document RAG solution targeting underserved small-to-mid-size firms.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary ICP: Small-to-Mid Tax/Accounting Firms

| Characteristic | Specification |
|----------------|---------------|
| **Firm Size** | 2-50 professionals |
| **Revenue** | $500K - $25M annually |

## Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Principles

1. **Value-Based:** 70-80% time savings = 10x ROI at our price
2. **Accessible:** 10x cheaper than CCH/Bloomberg
3. **Simple:** No complex tiers or per-query charges
4. **Scalable:** Per-user or per-firm models

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Target | Price | Features |
|------|--------|-------|----------|
| **Starter** | Solo practitioners | $99/month | 1 user, 50 docs, 500 queries |
| **Professional** | Small firms (2-10) | $199/user/month | Unlimited docs, queries, team collaboration |
| **Team** | Mid firms (11-50) | $149/user/month | Volume discount, priority support, API access |
| **Enterprise** | Corporate/Large | Custom | SSO, compliance, dedicated success |

| Solution | Monthly Cost | Our Advantage |

## Go-To-Market Phases

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-6)

**Objective:** Validate product-market fit with 50 paying customers

**Activities:**
| Activity | Timeline | Success Metric |
|----------|----------|----------------|
| Beta launch with 20 firms | Month 1-2 | NPS > 40 |
| Iterate based on feedback | Month 2-3 | Feature completeness |
| Launch Starter tier | Month 3 | 25 paying customers |
| Launch Professional tier | Month 4 | 50 paying customers |
| Case studies from beta | Month 5-6 | 3 published case studies |

**Channels:**
- Direct outreach to CPA networks
- LinkedIn content (tax research tips)
- Tax Twitter/X community
- Local CPA society presentations

**Budget:** $10-20K/month (mostly founder time + ads)

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 7-12)

**Objective:** Scale to $100K MRR

**Activities:**
| Activity | Timeline | Success Metric |
|----------|----------|----------------|
| Content marketing engine | Month 7+ | 10K organic visits/month |
| Integration partnerships | Month 8+ | 2 integrations live |
| Referral program launch | Month 9 | 20% of new customers from referrals |
| Team tier launch | Month 10 | 10 mid-size firm customers |
| First conference presence | Month 11 | 50 qualified leads |

**Channels:**
- SEO (tax research keywords)
- Webinars with CPE credits
- CPA association partnerships
- Integration marketplaces (QuickBooks, etc.)

**Budget:** $30-50K/month

### Phase 3: Scale (Year 2+)

**Objective:** $500K+ MRR, market leadership

**Activities:**
- Enterprise sales team
- International expansion (UK, Canada, Australia)
- Strategic partnerships (accounting software)
- Product expansion (compliance, planning)

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|---------------------|
| **Enterprise competitors add RAG** | Move fast, build switching costs through firm knowledge base |
| **AI accuracy concerns** | Citation-grounding, human review workflows, accuracy metrics |
| **Long sales cycles** | Free trials, low-friction onboarding, quick wins |
| **Customer concentration** | Diversify ICP segments, no customer >5% of revenue |
| **Regulatory changes** | Monitor, adapt quickly, make it a feature |
